Hyatt Hotels Corporation Product Management Internship

Timeline: June 2023 - August 2023

Title: Digital Product Management Intern

Skills: User Experience Design, JIRA, Product Roadmapping, Market Research

Collaborators: Senior Product Mangers, Ios/Android Engineers, UX Researchers

Overview

During my internship, I focused on solving a key usability challenge: the hotel details page (page containing information related to that hotel) was structured in a way that led to user confusion and fatigue, making it harder for travelers to find essential booking information. My role was to uncover the root causes of this friction and develop a solution that streamlined the booking experience. Through user research, competitor analysis, and data-driven insights, I identified opportunities to optimize information hierarchy, improve navigation, and enhance the overall usability of the page.

My Impact

  • Conducted competitor analysis, card sorting exercises, and market research to identify pain points in navigation and content organization.
  • Helped define an adaptive content approach, prioritizing key booking details while allowing niche travelers to access additional information when needed
  • Presented research findings and usability improvements to stakeholders, influencing key design decisions

Lessons and Takeaways

This experience reinforced the importance of user-centered design in solving complex usability challenges. I learned how to leverage research methodologies to inform design decisions, collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams, and translate data into actionable improvements. Most importantly, I saw firsthand how thoughtful design can transform a frustrating experience into an intuitive and engaging digital interaction.
