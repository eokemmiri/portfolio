    About Me

    Welcome! I'm Elizabeth Okemmiri.


    I’m passionate about understanding users and their individual experiences to best solve complex problems.


    I’m currently a senior at Northwestern University pursuing a major in Computer Science and a certificate in Design.


    I enjoy collaborating across cross-functional teams, communicating my ideas to different audiences, and incorporating feedback from users throughout the design process. Whether I’m redesigning Hyatt’s hotel description page or creating an educational game to encourage logical decision making for elementary school students, I’m passionate about transforming ambiguous problems into focused solutions while prioritizing user needs.


    When I’m not studying or analyzing a new problem, you can find me making unique recipes, reading, or catching up on a Podcast! I look forward to starting my career in consulting to forge meaningful connections and explore different industries.
